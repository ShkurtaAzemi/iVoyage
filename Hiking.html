    Hiking Kosova

    WALK TO MAJA KUNORAVE-PASHTRIK

    14 January 2018

    On Sunday January 14th hiking to beautiful Gjakova mountain peak of "Maja e Kunorave". The level of difficulty is moderate with aprox 13 km (ascent and descent) forest trail. To reserve your seat please call 045 834 141 (viber). We will start with a bus at 0700 am Sunday morning in front of the tennis courts in Pristina and come back at the same place around 22 00 pm.

    MAJESTIC HIKE TO SKARPA 2474M

    13 February 2018

    On Tuesday February 13th hiking to beautiful Opoja mountain in Sharr of Dragash. We will hike to it's peak 2474m and then visit it's lakes on the other side of the peak (Tetova side). To reserve your seat please call 049 248 716 or 045 834 141. To register, please come to the Tennis courts Prishtina in Sunny Hill from 5-7 pm every day except Sundays.

    HIKE AND SWIM IN MIRUSHA

    25 June 2018

    Short Trekk and swim time in Mirusha gorge lakes of Klina / Malisheva.The level of difficulty is easy to moderate with approx. 5 km trekk. To reserve your seat please call 049 248 716 (viber). We will start with a bus from Pristina at 0900 am (25 Jun.) morning (sharp) in front of the tennis courts and come back at the same place around 1900 pm. Please also bring your swimming wear.
