The St. Anne's Church

The St. Anne's Church on the Danube is a monument of cultural heritage in the Danube, Gjilan, Kosovo. The Church of Saint Ann is built in 1938, the same year when the village mosque was built. This church is the only Albanian Catholic church in this municipality. It is located close to three state borders: Kosovo, Serbia and Macedonia, has a very good position.

Pogragja (Kalaja e Pogragjës)

The fortress is 10 kilometers south-east of Gjilan. It was built on a hill overlooking the confluence of a stream in Binačka Morava, and dates back to 14th century. The fortress is situated on a hill that rises about 50 meters above the river valley. It consists of the Lower Town, surrounded by walls and a more preserved citadel (Upper Town) at the top of the hill.
