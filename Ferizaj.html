The Public Library of Ferizaj

The City Library or the Public Library of Ferizaj / Urosevac is a very special feature of this city, as it relates to the oldest building in the city which is said to have been raised by Turks in 1912 as the city's capital.

The Mosque and Church share the same yard

Religious tolerance in Kosovo can best be measured on the basis of this photography. The Mosque and Church share the same yard in Ferizaj for several years now. This is rare in the world and perhaps unique.

The Mill

Half-century mill of Ferizaj was built at the time of the Ottoman Empire. This mill is built in the course of the nerodime river, where the physical divide of the bifurcation occurs on its two branches, one branch flowing into the Black Sea, and the other branch flows into the Aegean Sea.

Bifurcation in Ferizaj

Bifurcation is a natural phenomenon occurring when one river is divided into two streams which then pour into different seas. There are only two such phenomena occurring in the world.
One of these phenomena is in Nerodime River, where one stream pours into the Aegean Sea through Vardar River; while the other stream pours into the Black Sea through Sitnica River and Iber River. This extraordinary natural phenomenon can be visited every day.
